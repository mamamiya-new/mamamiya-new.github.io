✨ Popular Questions and Answers

  • How to transfer tracks from other music apps?

    You can easily do it by selecting share option on the other app and selecting Mamamiya on the sharing list. You can also transfer multiple files at once, app supports that.

  • How do I add music to the player?

    You can user several import options: Files app, music from other apps, cloud storage, wifi transfer from friends and Airdrop, Mamamiya also supports any music you download on your device

  • What file formats are supported?

    Mamamiya supports almost any file format you upload into it: mp3, mp4, wav, Flac, m4a and more!

  • Does Mamamiya support songs ordering / sorting ?

    Yes, you can order music manually or sort by new/old, a-z, artist name and more!

  • Can I stream music to other devices?

    Yes, streaming is supported by Airplay on iOS and Chromecast on Android
